### Ronaldo: The Ultimate Finisher
Ronaldo is built entirely around scoring goals. His signature moves allow him to execute powerful shots from distance, leaving goalkeepers with minimal reaction time. When playing as Ronaldo, you should position yourself near the attacking third and wait for playmakers to feed you the ball. Use the standard dribble control (**Q**) to create a brief window of space, align your character toward an open corner of the net, and release a high-power shot.

### Luffy / Gear 5: Unmatched Mobility
Gear 5 Luffy focuses on chaotic movement. His rubbery abilities allow him to bounce over sliding defenders, making traditional defensive counter-strategies ineffective. If an opponent attempts to slide-tackle you (**E**), activate Luffy's mobility moves to glide past them. This creates immediate odd-man rushes and forces the opposing goalkeeper to leave their line to challenge you.

<Callout type="tip" title="Combos Tip">
When using Gear 5 Luffy, avoid dribbling in straight lines. Utilize his erratic bouncing animations to move diagonally across the field, baiting defenders into wasting their slide cooldowns before you make your final run toward the goal.
</Callout>
