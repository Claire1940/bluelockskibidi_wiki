### Advanced Movement Techniques

1. **The Feint**: Use **Q** to start a dribble, then immediately change direction with your movement keys to bait a defender's slide.
2. **The Aerial Clear**: If the ball is in the air, jumping before using **Left Click** provides more power and height to your clearance.
3. **Stamina Management**: Running and using skills drains your bar. Save at least 20% of your stamina for a defensive slide or an emergency dribble.
