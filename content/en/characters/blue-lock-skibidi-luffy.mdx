<Section icon={Sparkles} title="Awakened Gear 5 Luffy Abilities">

Activating the Gear 5 awakening transforms your playstyle, granting access to high-impact abilities that can turn the tide of any match. The awakening state emphasizes overwhelming offensive power and unpredictable movement patterns.

The two main abilities in this mode are Red Hawk and Dawn Rocket. Both moves require careful aiming and positioning to maximize their effectiveness on the pitch.

<Callout type="warning" title="Awakening Limitations">
You cannot easily pick up the ball during the active bouncing animation of Dawn Rocket. Use this move for positioning rather than direct dribbling.
</Callout>

| Awakening Move | Skill Type | Key Effect | Strategic Value |
|----------------|------------|------------|-----------------|
| Red Hawk | Offensive | Long blast cutscene | High scoring potential |
| Dawn Rocket | Mobility | Vertical bouncing | Repositioning & Escape |

Red Hawk is a signature scoring move that triggers a dedicated cutscene animation. When activated, it fires a long, high-velocity blast that drives the ball forward. If you aim this skill directly at the opponent's goal, the sheer force of the blast can easily bypass standard keeper defenses. 

Dawn Rocket, on the other hand, is a mobility tool that causes your character to bounce repeatedly upwards and downwards. While this makes you extremely difficult to hit or tackle, it limits your ability to dribble the ball along the ground. Use it to escape high-pressure situations or to position yourself for an incoming aerial pass.
