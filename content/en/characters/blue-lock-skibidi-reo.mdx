<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Game Controls and Mechanics">

Success on the field is determined by your timing and positioning. While special abilities can turn the tide of a match, mastering the foundational movement and defensive inputs is what separates average players from MVPs.

| Action | Desktop Key | Function | Optimal Match Timing |
|--------|-------------|----------|----------------------|
| **Shoot / Clear** | Left Mouse Button | Kicks ball forward | Face your target before shooting; clear when in your own box |
| **Slide Tackle** | E | Performs a slide | Use after the opponent commits to a specific dribble direction |
| **Dribble** | Q | Activates ball control | Use when a defender approaches to shield the ball |
| **GK Dive** | Q | Dives toward the ball | Wait for the ball to leave the striker's foot before diving |
| **Request Ball** | R | Signals for a pass | Activate when you have completely broken away into open space |
| **Skills 1–5** | 1, 2, 3, 4, 5 | Activates character moves | Check range and possession requirements before pressing |

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Get in Position">
    Do not bunch up with your teammates. Spread out across the pitch to create passing lanes and open up space.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Shield and Dribble">
    When a defender approaches, press **Q** to activate your dribble. This protects the ball and allows you to look for an open teammate.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Execute the Shot">
    Face the net, charge your shot with **Left Click**, and aim for the corners of the goal where the goalkeeper cannot easily reach.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Goalkeeper Guide: Defending the Net">

Playing as a goalkeeper in **blue lock skibidi** requires extreme patience and quick reflexes. A single premature dive can leave the entire net wide open for an easy tap-in goal.

| Goalkeeper Strategy | Action | Expected Result |
|---------------------|--------|-----------------|
| **Center Positioning** | Stand in the middle of the goal line | Keeps both the left and right corners within diving range |
| **Delayed Diving** | Press **Q** only after the ball is shot | Prevents attackers from baiting your dive with dribble fakes |
| **Wide Angle Defense** | Shift toward the near post on wing attacks | Reduces the shooter's available scoring angles |
| **Safe Distribution** | Face an open teammate before kicking | Avoids turning the ball over directly to waiting strikers |
