### King's Chop Shot & Feint Mechanics
The King's Chop Shot is Barou's signature move. Tapping the ability key twice executes a rapid cut that allows you to change direction quickly while maintaining momentum. Alternatively, holding the button charges a powerful shot. The key to mastering this move is the feint: you can cancel the charge animation midway to trick defenders who have already committed to a block or tackle.

### Devour & Shadow Dribbling
Devour acts as an instantaneous transition tool. When activated, Barou performs a quick, aggressive dash that allows him to steal the ball or bypass a defender immediately. Shadow Dribbling enhances this by providing superior ball control during high-speed runs, making it difficult for standard defenders to dispossess you.

### Ankle Break Utility
One of Barou's most powerful passive traits is his ability to break defender ankles even when he does not possess the ball. By running close to defenders while off-ball, you can trigger a brief stun animation on them, opening up passing lanes for your teammates or allowing you to receive a pass in space.
