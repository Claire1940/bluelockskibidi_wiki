<Section icon={Shield} title="Goalkeeper Masterclass">

Playing goalkeeper is one of the most demanding roles in the game. It requires patience, precise positioning, and a deep understanding of how various character abilities travel through the air.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Center Your Position">
    Always return to the center of your goal line. Moving too far to one side makes it easy for strikers to shoot into the open corner.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Track the Ball, Not the Player">
    Focus your eyes on the ball's trajectory rather than the attacker's body movements. Many styles use trick animations to bait early dives.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Delay Your Dive">
    Only press **Q** after the ball has left the striker's foot. An early dive allows the opponent to simply walk the ball into the net.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Execute Safe Clearances">
    Once you catch or block the ball, turn your character toward the wings or an open teammate before clicking to kick. Never clear the ball straight up the middle.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Goalkeeper Perk">
Characters like Gagamaru have passive traits that increase their dive reach. If you are designated as the team's primary keeper, make sure to equip a defense-oriented style.
</Callout>

</Section>
