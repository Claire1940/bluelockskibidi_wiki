Even with an optimized setup, you may occasionally encounter input issues. The table below outlines common control problems and their recommended solutions. If you experience hardware-specific difficulties, refer to the [Roblox Support Center](https://support.roblox.com) for device troubleshooting.

| Issue | Potential Cause | Recommended Solution |
|---|---|---|
| **Input Delay** | High network latency or V-Sync enabled | Disable V-Sync in video settings and check your connection |
| **Controller Not Working** | Driver conflict or undetected device | Restart the client and ensure Steam Input is enabled |
| **Stuck Keys** | Sticky keys enabled or hardware issue | Disable Windows Sticky Keys and clean your keyboard switches |
| **Stamina Depleting Fast** | Continuous sprinting | Release the sprint key when off the ball or during slow plays |
