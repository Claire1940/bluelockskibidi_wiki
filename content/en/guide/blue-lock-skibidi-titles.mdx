Advanced players often use the **Q** dribble to bait defenders into a slide. By pressing Q just as a defender initiates their **E** slide, you can often maneuver around them, leaving the goal wide open for a clear shot. This "bait-and-switch" tactic is vital in the higher-ranked lobbies of 2026.
